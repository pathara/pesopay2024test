Significance and impact of digital payment solutions in the hospitality industry

by: Kezia Shane Reyes

DIGITALIZING HOTEL BOOKINGS WITH SECURE AND EASY PAYMENT OPTIONS

The hospitality industry, known for being the backbone of the economy in many tourism countries, is driven by a digital transformation— continuous adaptation to contactless payment transactions. Over the years, the industry is becoming highly competitive to reach guests’ changing expectations and preferences. This includes providing a more user-friendly experience on online booking that strives to keep up with the latest tech trends.

In the Philippines, resorts and hotels like Okada Manila, Solaire Resort, and Canyon Cove Hotel and Spa utilize digital payment solutions for three primary reasons:

  1. Convenience and Accessibility - Guests can now effortlessly book accommodations by providing them easy access to a range of services that suit their budget and preferences. This simplifies the method of arranging their travel and payment processing.
  2. Enhanced Security - Guests that use hotel booking websites feel more confident paying digitally knowing that their sensitive and confidential information is protected by the additional security measures featured in the digital payment process. This lowers the risk of fraud and data breaching.
  3. Operational Efficiency - Hotels and resorts may automate their financial operations through digital payment gateways to save time and resources on cash handling. Through this, hotel staff may focus on improving guest services rather than handling transactions.

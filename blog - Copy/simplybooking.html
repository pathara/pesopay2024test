Foreword

AsiaPay’s payment gateways—PayDollar, PesoPay and BimoPay—have been integrated into simpleBooking’s online booking engine to provide an automated and secure way for hotel customers to make an upfront payment when booking online or on mobile.

Now hotels can accept a wider range of payment methods, including international credit cards, internet banking, regional digital wallets, Buy Now Pay Later, and over the counter. This eliminates the need for costly terminals and manual payment processing during customer check-in. In addition, upfront payments will also reduce cancellations or no-shows for hotels across Asia.
